Dieter Rams

Introduction

Welcome to our tribute to Dieter Rams, one of the most influential designers of the twentieth century. Through this site, we aspire to shed light on Rams' life, work, and enduring impact on the world of design. From physical products to digital solutions, Rams' principles guide modern design philosophies. As you navigate through this site, we hope you will gain a deeper appreciation for Rams' legacy, as well as the beauty and functionality of his works.
